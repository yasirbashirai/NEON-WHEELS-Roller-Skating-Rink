The Future We're Building

More Than A Rink —
A Pathway to Possibility

Neon Wheels is being created to provide safe recreation, education, mentorship, wellness, and youth development for Lake County.

Safe Youth Recreation

A fun, inclusive environment where youth skate, build confidence, and make positive choices.

Education & Empowerment

Tutoring, STEM learning, and youth empowerment workshops for ages 5–18 and beyond.

Mentorship & Leadership

Positive mentorship and leadership pathways for teens and young adults.

Health & Wellness

Promoting physical activity, family wellness, and lifelong healthy habits.

Family-Friendly Events

Hosting birthday parties, school outings, and community celebrations.

Community Connection

Building stronger families and lasting community bonds under one roof.

Why This Impact Matters

Positive Alternatives. Supportive Relationships. A Safe Place To Grow.

Neon Wheels is designed to give young people positive alternatives, supportive relationships, and a safe environment where they can grow, connect, and thrive.

For Youth

Access to recreation, mentorship, STEM, financial literacy, leadership development, and safe social opportunities.

For Families

Affordable entertainment, wellness events, family connection, and a safe destination to create lasting memories.

For Communities

Stronger partnerships, reduced isolation, positive youth engagement, local jobs, and long-term community pride.

Impact Timeline

A Clear Roadmap From Launch To Long-Term Impact

Phase 1
