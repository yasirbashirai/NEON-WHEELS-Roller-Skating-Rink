Programs & Services

Programs &
Services

Programs that inspire, empower & transform lives

Neon Wheels will be more than a place to skate. It's a safe, vibrant community hub where youth, families, and adults can enjoy recreation, build life skills, receive support, and create positive memories in one welcoming space.

Support Our Mission Explore Programs
6 Program Categories

One Facility. Endless Opportunities.

Youth & Teen Programs

Safe, supervised programs that give young people positive places to gather, grow, and thrive.

  • Safe Haven Youth Recreation — Structured environment where youth can skate, socialize, and stay engaged after school and weekends.
  • Teen Night & Positive Social Events — Supervised events to connect, enjoy music, and build healthy friendships.
  • Youth Mentorship Program — Positive mentorship that builds confidence, leadership, responsibility.
  • Leaders in Training Workforce Program — Hands-on workforce readiness teaching customer service, teamwork, leadership.

Education & Life Skills

Helping youth build confidence and long-term success.

  • Financial Literacy Program — Teaches budgeting, saving, entrepreneurship, banking, and smart financial decisions.
  • Future Builders STEM Initiative — Interactive STEM experiences introducing science, technology, engineering, and math.
  • Homework Help & Academic Support — A supportive learning space focused on school success.

Health, Wellness & Fitness

Promoting physical health, emotional wellness, and family connection.

  • Mind & Motion Wellness — Emotional wellness, stress relief, confidence-building, and movement programs.
  • Roller Fitness Classes — High-energy skating-based fitness for youth, adults, and families.
  • Family Wellness Nights — Events that encourage healthy family engagement and quality time.

Community & Family Services

Building stronger families and safer communities throughout Lake County.

  • Family Skate Sessions — Affordable skating sessions that bring families together safely.
  • Community Outreach Events — Resource fairs, school partnerships, and community engagement.
  • Violence Prevention & Safe Spaces — Positive alternatives that reduce youth violence.
  • Inclusive Skate Sessions — Specialized sessions for individuals with different abilities and sensory needs.

Events & Facility Services

Creating unforgettable experiences for the community.

  • Birthday Parties — Fun birthday experiences with skating, music, food, and celebration space.
  • Private Event Rentals — Rentals for schools, churches, corporations, and families.
  • School & Youth Group Outings — Group skating experiences for schools, camps, and youth groups.
  • Fundraising Events — Community fundraising opportunities for schools, churches, and nonprofits.

Recreation & Entertainment Experience

Keeping families engaged, active, and entertained.

  • Concession & Café Services — Family-friendly food, snacks, and refreshments.
  • Arcade & Game Zone — Interactive games and entertainment for all ages.
  • Music, Theme Nights & Live Entertainment — DJ nights, themed skate sessions, and family entertainment.

One Facility. Many Pathways to Impact.

With donor support, Neon Wheels will provide recreation, mentorship, financial literacy, workforce readiness, STEM education, wellness programming, and community connection — all under one roof.
