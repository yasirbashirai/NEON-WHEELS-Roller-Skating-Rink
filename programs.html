Programs & Services

Programs That Inspire, Empower, and Transform Lives

Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center will be more than a place to skate. It will be a safe, vibrant community hub where youth, families, and adults can enjoy recreation, build life skills, receive support, and create positive memories in one welcoming space.

♥ Support Our Mission Explore Programs →
👥

Youth & Teen Programs

Safe, supervised programs that give young people positive places to gather, grow, and thrive.

  • Safe Haven Youth Recreation — Safe, structured environment where youth can skate, socialize, and stay engaged in positive activities after school, weekends, and school breaks.
  • Teen Night & Positive Social Events — Supervised events designed to give teens a safe place to connect, enjoy music, and build healthy friendships.
  • Youth Mentorship Program — Positive mentorship opportunities that encourage confidence, leadership, responsibility, and personal growth.
  • Leaders in Training Workforce Program — Hands-on workforce readiness program teaching customer service, teamwork, leadership, and workplace responsibility.
🎓

Education & Life Skills Programs

Helping youth build confidence and long-term success.

  • Financial Literacy Program — Teaches budgeting, saving, entrepreneurship, banking, and smart financial decision-making.
  • Future Builders STEM Initiative — Interactive STEM experiences introducing science, technology, engineering, and math through creative learning.
  • Homework Help & Academic Support — A supportive learning space focused on school success and educational encouragement.
💚

Health, Wellness & Fitness Programs

Promoting physical health, emotional wellness, and family connection.

  • Mind & Motion Wellness — Programs focused on emotional wellness, stress relief, confidence-building, and movement.
  • Roller Fitness Classes — High-energy skating-based fitness classes for youth, adults, and families.
  • Family Wellness Nights — Events designed to encourage healthy family engagement and quality time together.
🤝

Community & Family Services

Building stronger families and safer communities throughout Lake County.

  • Family Skate Sessions — Affordable skating sessions that bring families together in a safe environment.
  • Community Outreach Events — Resource fairs, school partnerships, and community engagement programs.
  • Violence Prevention & Safe Space Initiatives — Positive alternatives that help reduce youth violence and provide safe recreational opportunities.
  • Inclusive Skate Sessions — Specialized sessions welcoming individuals with different abilities and sensory needs.
📅

Events & Facility Services

Creating unforgettable experiences for the community.

  • Birthday Parties — Fun birthday experiences with skating, music, food, and celebration space.
  • Private Event Rentals — Facility rentals for schools, churches, corporations, and family events.
  • School & Youth Group Outings — Group skating experiences for schools, camps, and youth organizations.
  • Fundraising Events — Community fundraising opportunities for schools, churches, and nonprofits.
🍿

Food, Fun & Entertainment

Keeping families engaged, active, and entertained.

  • Concession & Café Services — Family-friendly food, snacks, and refreshments.
  • Arcade & Game Zone — Interactive games and entertainment area for all ages.
  • Music, Theme Nights & Live Entertainment — DJ nights, themed skate sessions, and family entertainment experiences.

One Facility. Many Pathways to Impact.

With donor support, Neon Wheels will provide recreation, mentorship, financial literacy, workforce readiness, STEM education, wellness programming, and community connection under one roof.
