Leadership Driving the Vision

Meet the Visionary Leaders

The passionate change-makers behind Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center — leaders committed to youth empowerment, community transformation, and creating brighter futures for the families of Lake County.

Barbara Washington

Founder & Executive Director

Founder & Executive Director

Visionary leader committed to youth empowerment and community transformation.

Barbara Washington is a dynamic business leader and dedicated community advocate with a strong foundation in supply chain management and entrepreneurship. As the owner of a successful freight brokerage firm, she brings strategic insight, operational excellence, and proven leadership to the mission of Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center.

With formal education and certification in Supply Chain Management, Barbara has built a career centered on efficiency, growth, and results — skills she now leverages to create meaningful opportunities for underserved communities.

Her passion for service is demonstrated through her active involvement in youth programs at Christian Love Baptist Church and her leadership roles on nonprofit boards focused on education, STEM advancement, and workforce development.

As the visionary behind Neon Wheels, Barbara is building more than a recreational facility — she is leading the development of a transformative community hub designed to inspire, empower, and uplift the next generation.

Director of Community Engagement

Dedicated leader committed to strengthening communities and empowering future generations.

Edward Houston is a seasoned community leader, minister, and advocate with more than 30 years of experience uplifting individuals and families. Known for his strong moral leadership and unwavering commitment to community development, he brings a powerful combination of spiritual guidance, real estate expertise, and civic engagement to the Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center mission.

With a background in Liberal Arts and experience as a licensed realtor, Edward has played a vital role in promoting housing stability and supporting sustainable community growth. His professional and community-based work reflects a deep understanding of the needs of underserved populations and a commitment to creating lasting solutions.

Edward currently serves on the board of the North Chicago Public Library and has contributed to numerous nonprofit organizations, helping guide strategic, financial, and operational initiatives that strengthen communities.

His lifelong dedication to youth development, education, and empowerment makes him a driving force behind the vision of Neon Wheels. Edward's leadership is rooted in purpose and passion — focused on creating opportunity, building stronger communities, and shaping a brighter future for the next generation.

Sybria Davis

Director of Community Engagement

Director of Community Engagement

Building meaningful partnerships and advancing community impact.

Sybria Davis is a Licensed Social Worker and seasoned mental health professional with nearly two decades of experience supporting youth, individuals, and families. She holds a Master of Social Work from the University of Nevada–Reno and brings a strong foundation of clinical expertise paired with a compassionate, results-driven approach.

Throughout her career, Sybria has served in nonprofit organizations, clinical environments, and private practice, with a specialized focus on adolescents and young adults facing behavioral, emotional, and life challenges. Her person-centered methodology emphasizes resilience, self-confidence, and sustainable personal growth, creating safe, supportive spaces where individuals can thrive.

Sybria is deeply committed to strengthening communities by empowering individuals with the tools and resources needed to overcome adversity and achieve long-term success. In her role with Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center, she leads community engagement efforts, builds strategic partnerships, and helps design impactful programs that promote youth development, emotional wellness, and positive life outcomes.
