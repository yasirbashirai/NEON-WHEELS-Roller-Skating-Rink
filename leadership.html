From Our Leadership Team

We Could No Longer Sit Idle

After hearing about the tragic incidents involving teenagers charged in the murder of a restaurant and nightclub owner in Waukegan, along with the heartbreaking killing of a teacher while driving, we realized our communities could no longer afford to sit idle.

We knew we had to take action. Neon Wheels was born from the desire to help save our youth, provide a safe place to belong, and create opportunities for young people to thrive, turn their lives around, and become successful.

MSW, LSW – Director of Youth & Family Services

Empowering youth through emotional wellness, mentorship, and resilience-building.

Sybria Davis, MSW, LSW, is a Licensed Social Worker and experienced mental health professional with nearly 20 years of experience supporting youth, individuals, and families.

She holds a Master of Social Work from the University of Nevada–Reno and has worked across nonprofit organizations, clinical settings, and private practice with a strong focus on adolescent and young adult development.

Sybria is passionate about empowering youth through emotional wellness, mentorship, resilience-building, and community support.

At Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center, she helps lead community engagement, strategic partnerships, and youth-focused programs designed to create positive life outcomes and strengthen Lake County, Illinois families and communities.
