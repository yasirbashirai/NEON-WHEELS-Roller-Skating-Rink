Our Mission

Neon Wheels exists to provide a safe, positive, and inspiring environment where youth and families can grow through recreation, mentorship, education, fitness, creativity, and community connection.

We are committed to helping reduce youth violence, strengthen families, improve mental wellness, and create opportunities that empower the next generation throughout Lake County.

A PLACE to SKATE.
A PURPOSE to INSPIRE.
A COMMUNITY to TRANSFORM.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in Lake County has access to safe spaces, positive role models, healthy activities, and opportunities that inspire confidence, leadership, and purpose.

Neon Wheels aims to become more than a skating rink — we are building a community destination focused on youth development, violence prevention, family engagement, and long-term community impact.

The Reality

The Need in Our Communities

Too many young people in Lake County are growing up surrounded by violence, isolation, and limited opportunities. Neon Wheels was created to help answer that need by providing hope, mentorship, and positive experiences.

Recent Youth Community Violence Highlights
  • Waukegan: Teen charged in fatal restaurant stabbing
  • Zion: Multiple youth shootings and violent incidents
  • North Chicago: Increase in gun violence and juvenile crime across neighborhoods

Our youth need more than programs — they need hope, safety, mentorship, opportunity, and a community that believes in them.

Founder's Story

Why We Started Neon Wheels

Neon Wheels was born out of a passion to see our youth safe, inspired, and empowered. Growing up in Lake County, we saw too many talented young people lose their way simply because they lacked access to positive environments, mentorship, and dream-big opportunities.

We created Neon Wheels to be the place we wish we had — a place where kids can skate, laugh, grow, build confidence, and dream big. Our mission is simple: change lives, strengthen families, and build brighter futures.

— Edward Houston & Barbara Washington-Houston

Founders, Neon Wheels
