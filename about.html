About Neon Wheels

More Than A Rink.
A Movement For
Youth & Families.

Neon Wheels Roller Skating Rink & Fun Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and purpose-driven youth and family entertainment destination dedicated to inspiring, empowering, and strengthening communities throughout Lake County, Illinois.

Our mission is to provide a safe, uplifting, and transformational environment where youth and families can thrive through recreation, mentorship, leadership development, STEM education, financial literacy, health and wellness, creative arts, and meaningful community engagement.

More than a roller-skating rink, Neon Wheels serves as a community hub where young people discover their potential, families build lasting connections, and lives are transformed through positive experiences, educational opportunities, and supportive relationships.

Our Vision

A Premier Youth & Family Destination In Illinois

Our vision is to create one of the premier youth and family entertainment destinations in Illinois — a place where fun, mentorship, education, inspiration, and opportunity work together to positively impact generations of youth and families.

Neon Wheels was created to provide youth with a place where they feel safe, encouraged, inspired, supported, and empowered to become successful leaders, entrepreneurs, students, athletes, creators, and future professionals.
