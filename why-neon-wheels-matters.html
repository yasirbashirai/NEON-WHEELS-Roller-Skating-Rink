A Call To Save Our Youth

Why Neon Wheels Matters

Recent violent incidents involving teenagers and young adults across eastern Lake County, Illinois have deeply impacted families, schools, and neighborhoods. Neon Wheels was created to provide hope, safety, mentorship, opportunity, and positive alternatives for the next generation.

The Reality

A Community At A Crossroads

Recent violent incidents involving teenagers and young adults across eastern Lake County, Illinois communities have deeply impacted families, schools, and neighborhoods. These tragedies became a painful reminder that our communities could no longer sit idle.

The Challenge

  • Youth violence and teen gun violence
  • Community trauma affecting families and schools
  • Lack of safe after-school and weekend environments
  • Limited access to positive youth engagement opportunities

The Risk

  • Exposure to gangs, drugs, and unsafe influences
  • Vulnerable youth left without structure during critical hours
  • Fewer opportunities for mentorship and personal development
  • Greater pressure on families, schools, and neighborhoods

The Need

  • Safe recreational spaces
  • Mentorship and violence prevention programs
  • Affordable family entertainment
  • Positive environments where youth can grow and thrive
From Our Leadership Team

We Could No Longer Sit Idle

After hearing about the tragic incidents involving teenagers charged in the murder of a restaurant and nightclub owner, along with the heartbreaking killing of a teacher while driving, we realized our communities could no longer afford to sit idle.

We knew we had to take action. Neon Wheels was born from the desire to help save our youth, provide a safe place to belong, and create opportunities for young people to thrive, turn their lives around, and become successful.

The Solution

More Than A Skating Rink

Neon Wheels is more than a skating rink. It is a safe haven, a youth development center, a family entertainment destination, and a community solution for Lake County, Illinois.

Recreation & Fitness

Roller skating, movement, music, and active entertainment that encourages healthy fun, confidence, and positive social connection.

Mentorship & Leadership

Programs designed to help youth build character, develop leadership skills, make better choices, and believe in their future.

Creativity & Innovation

STEM, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, workforce readiness, and creative learning opportunities that expose youth to success pathways.
