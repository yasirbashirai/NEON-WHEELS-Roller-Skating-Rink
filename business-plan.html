Executive Summary

A nonprofit facility designed for impact and sustainability

Neon Wheels brings together roller skating entertainment, STEM and workforce programming, mentorship, and safe youth engagement to serve families across Lake County.

Market Opportunity

Strong need for structured, safe youth spaces

The region needs more recreation, after-school engagement, crime prevention support, and community-based programming that drives measurable outcomes.

Business Model

Diversified revenue for long-term stability

A hybrid nonprofit structure combines earned income, program revenue, grants, donations, and sponsorships to reduce dependency on any single funding source.

Target Market

  • Youth (ages 5–18)
  • Families
  • Schools & youth organizations
  • Community programs

Local Demand Drivers

  • Limited safe recreational spaces
  • Youth crime prevention initiatives
  • Demand for structured after-school programs

Competitive Advantage

  • Multi-purpose skating + education facility
  • Nonprofit status unlocks grant funding
  • Strong community impact positioning
