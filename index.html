More Than A Rink. A Movement.

A Safe Haven For Every Kid In Lake County, Illinois

Neon Wheels is more than a skating rink. It is a safe haven, a youth development center, a family entertainment destination, and a community solution.

Recreation & Fitness

Roller skating, movement, music, and active entertainment that encourages healthy fun, confidence, and positive social connection.

Mentorship & Leadership

Programs designed to help youth build character, develop leadership skills, make better choices, and believe in their future.

Creativity & Innovation

STEM, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, workforce readiness, and creative learning opportunities that expose youth to success pathways.

6 Program Areas. Endless Opportunities.

Programs That Build Futures

One facility. Many pathways to impact. Designed to inspire, empower, and transform lives for youth and families across Lake County, Illinois.

Skate & Stay Active

Promoting health, confidence, friendship, and active lifestyles through positive recreation.

Education & Tutoring

Academic support to help students succeed in school and in life through homework help and enrichment.

STEM & Innovation

Inspiring creativity, technology, and problem-solving for the careers and futures of tomorrow.

Mentorship & Leadership

Building character, confidence, and tomorrow's leaders through positive adult guidance.

Financial Literacy

Teaching smart money habits, entrepreneurship, and workforce readiness for a stronger future.

Community Engagement

Bringing families together and strengthening neighborhoods across Lake County, Illinois.
